The articles below are hosted by AMD.

## Performance profiling on AMD GPUs — three-part series

1. [Performance Profiling on AMD GPUs – Part 1: Foundations](https://rocm.blogs.amd.com/software-tools-optimization/profiling-guide/intro/README.html) — profiling concepts and introduction
2. [Performance Profiling on AMD GPUs – Part 2: Basic Usage](https://rocm.blogs.amd.com/software-tools-optimization/profiling-guide/novice/README.html) — first steps with ROCm profiling tools and basic usage
3. [Performance Profiling on AMD GPUs – Part 3: Advanced Usage](https://rocm.blogs.amd.com/software-tools-optimization/profiling-guide/advanced/README.html) — multi-device profiling and optimisation workflows
