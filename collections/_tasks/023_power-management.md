## Fit to programme


This was a proposed solution answering Task 023: Power management, behind [WP 1.1](/workpackages/workpackage-1/).



## Summary

This proposal explores simple methods for HPC system dynamic power
capping in response to external factors such as grid supply pricing
and spot carbon intensity.  We will study the control achievable from a
system perspective, and will also explore the feasibility of
dynamically controlling power based on external inputs, and seek to
demonstrate a prototype implementation using the COSMA HPC facility.

We will investigate multiple power control techniques, and study the
impact that these have on the scientific output of codes, drawing
pragmatic conclusions based around total cost of ownership and total
carbon produced.

We will showcase our findings at national and/or international
conferences, and will deliver a white paper to be hosted on the
SHAREing website.



## Outputs

A white paper for the SHAREing website, and presentations at
conferences, events or workshops.

We will also seek to deliver a working prototype system capable of
reducing power demand in response to carbon intensity or grid pricing,
and seek to place this into production should reliability be proved.
