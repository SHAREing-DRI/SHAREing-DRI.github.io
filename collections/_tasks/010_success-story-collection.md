## Fit to programme

This task has been identified by the working groups as part of the agenda behind [WP 1.3](/workpackages/workpackage-1).
The task number is 007.

## Description

To publish success stories (use cases) on the SHAREing portal. This Task should develop 
a standardized template for success stories and collect use cases from consortium 
testbeds. There should be various definition for success stories on the landing page:

• What elements should a success story include (e.g. problem, solution, outcomes, 
metrics)?

• How can success stories be presented to maximize engagement (e.g. 
visualisation, videos)?

The content should highlight both technical achievements and research impact.


## Outcomes

• Published collection of 3-5 detailed success stories on the portal.

• Multimedia content library (documents, videos)
